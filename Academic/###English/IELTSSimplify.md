## Accustom

1. **To**

   ```shell
   1. adj/v to v
   2. to noun/v-ing
   ```

2. **Do**

   ```shell
   1. do/don't v
   2. Do/Does/Did sub v?
   ```

3. So

4. Too

5. Even

6. **Of**

   ```shell
   1. A of B (所属/材料/抽象)
   2. part/some/many/half/most/none of noun (一部分)
   3. a lot/a kind/a cup/two pieces of noun (几个)
   ```
