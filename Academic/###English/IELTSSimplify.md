## Accustom

1. **To**

   ```shell
   1. adj/v to v
   2. to noun/v-ing
   ```

2. **Do**

   ```shell
   1. do/don't v
   2. Do/Does/Did sub v?
   ```

3. **So**

   ```shell
   1. So ... so
   2. so...
   3. so adj that
   ```

4. **Too**

   ```shell
   1. (Only) Too adj that
   2. ... too
   ```

5. **Even**

   ```shell
   1. ... even v...
   2. even adj-er
   - Even if/Even though/Even so 
   ```

6. **Of**

   ```shell
   1. A of B (所属/材料/抽象)
   2. part/some/many/half/most/none of noun (一部分)
   3. a lot/a kind/a cup/two pieces of noun (几个)
   ```

7. **For**

   ```shell
   1. for 名词/代词/动名词
   2. for 人/物
   3. for 时间/距离
   4. for 地点
   - Wait for/Look for/Pay for/Thanks for/As for me
   ```

8. **By**

   ```shell
   1. by 名词/动名词
   2. by 人/物
   3. by 时间
   4. be by 名词
   5. by 数量
   - By the way
   By the time
   By accident
   By mistake
   Step by step
   By oneself
   ```

9. With

10. All

11. **What**

    ```shell
    1. What (noun) ...?
    2. What (a/an) adj n (n v)!
    3. ... What n v ...
    - So what/What for/What if/What's up/What about 
    ```

12. **Why**

    ```shell
    1. Why are/did ...?
    2. why not/why dont we-you
    3. (the reason) why 主谓
    - That's why
    Why is that?
    Why so? 
    Why me?
    ```

13. **How**

    ```shell
    1. how do you ...?
    2. how 主谓
    3. how adj  be 主
    4. how be动词 人/物
    5. how adj 主谓!
    - How about...?
    How about a cup of coffee?
    How come?
    How come youre not at the party?
    Hows it going? 
    ```

14. When

15. While
